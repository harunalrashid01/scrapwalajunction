FAQ

Your Questions, Answered Honestly.

Everything you need to know before you sell your old devices to us.

Are you a scrap dealer (kabadiwala)?
No. We are an IT asset buyback and refurbishment company. Unlike scrap dealers who buy devices purely for their metal value, we professionally assess, refurbish, and resell devices for reuse. This allows us to pay you significantly more — especially for devices that are still functional.
Is my company data really safe?
Absolutely. Data safety is our biggest commitment. We perform certified, multi-pass data wiping on every device before it is resold or refurbished. You receive a written Data Wipe Certificate as official proof of destruction. Your business data never falls into the wrong hands.
How quickly can you pick up?
We offer same-day or next-day pickup across Mumbai. Simply call or WhatsApp us with your requirement and we'll confirm a time slot within hours.
What types of devices do you buy?
We primarily buy laptops and desktop computers. We also accept monitors, printers, servers, and other IT peripherals on request. Devices do not need to be in perfect working condition — we assess each device individually.
How do you decide the price you pay?
We assess each device individually based on its brand, model, age, specifications (RAM, storage, processor), and physical condition. Working devices fetch significantly higher prices. Non-working devices are valued based on their refurb or parts potential. We are always transparent about our valuation.
Do you offer service for small quantities?
Yes! There is no minimum quantity. We will come to your office whether you have 5 laptops or 500. We serve small businesses with the same professionalism as large corporations.
Do you provide documents after pickup?
Yes. After every job, we provide: (1) A Pickup Receipt listing every device collected with serial numbers, and (2) A Data Wipe Certificate confirming secure data destruction. These documents are useful for your IT audit trail and compliance records.
What if some of our devices are not working?
No problem. We buy non-working and damaged devices too, though at a lower value. Even broken laptops can have value for parts or materials. Send us details and we'll give you an honest assessment.
